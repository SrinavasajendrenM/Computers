Central Processing Unit (CPU / Processor)

The CPU is a miniaturized electronic component that can be referred to as the brain of the PC. It is responsible for two things: Carries out commands to make the hardware components perform actions. Processes data; controls the execution of a computer and which performs basic arithmetic and logical operations. Although the instructions performed by the CPU are relatively simple, the CPU can execute many millions of instructions every second. This is what makes the PC such a powerful tool.
