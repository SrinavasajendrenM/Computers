In this project, we will be exploring the computer system, specifically the pieces of hardware that make it up. Here are the main things that will be covered:

  • Central Processing Unit (CPU / Processor)
  • Motherboard (Mainboard / System Board / Logicboard / MoBo)

Let's get started on how the Computer looked before we deconstructed it...

Central Processing Unit

The CPU was one of the first parts our group had managed to pull out of the computer, and is commonly referred to as the "brains" of a computer. The CPU is also known as the processor. The CPU is responsible for executing a program. It is responsible for two things: - Carrying out commands to make the hardware components perform actions. - Processing data; controls the execution of a computer and which performs basic arithmetic and logical operations. Although the instructions performed by the CPU are relatively simple, the CPU can execute many millions of instructions every second. This is what makes the PC such a powerful tool.

Motherboard

Although we had managed to take out the CPU quickly, the motherboard took quite a while to detach, it is a printed circuit board that is known as the foundation of a computer. It allocates power and allows communication to the CPU, RAM, and all other computer hardware components.
